Latiff Mohidin

Latiff Mohidin is a leading modern painter and poet, from Malaysia. He often asked himself, "How does one advance the medium of painting, drawing and writing to suit circumstances outside Europe and America?" He was especially interested in the iconic forms and shapes of the landscape and architecture in Southeast Asia.
